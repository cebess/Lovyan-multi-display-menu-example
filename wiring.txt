ESP32 Pin Connections for Two Displays:

Common Pins (Shared between both displays):
- GPIO 18 (SCLK) -> SCL on both displays
- GPIO 23 (MOSI) -> SDA on both displays
- GND -> GND on both displays
- 3.3V -> VCC on both displays

Display 1:
- GPIO 2  -> DC
- GPIO 5  -> CS
- GPIO 4  -> RES
- GPIO 19 -> BLK (Backlight control - optional)
- GPIO 21 -> Encoder A
- GPIO 22 -> Encoder B
- GPIO 26 -> Encoder PUSH
- GPIO 27 -> KO Button

Display 2:
- GPIO 15 -> DC
- GPIO 14 -> CS
- GPIO 12 -> RES
- GPIO 13 -> BLK (Backlight control - optional)
- GPIO 32 -> Encoder A
- GPIO 33 -> Encoder B
- GPIO 25 -> Encoder PUSH
- GPIO 34 -> KO Button

Note: VCC can be 3.3V or 5V (the board accepts both)
